Our History

IKEA was founded in 1943 by Ingvar Kamprad in Sweden. Over the decades it grew from a mail-order catalogue into one of the world's best-known home furnishings retailers, known for flat-pack furniture people assemble themselves.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a better everyday life for the many people, offering well-designed, functional home furnishings at prices so low that as many people as possible can afford them.
