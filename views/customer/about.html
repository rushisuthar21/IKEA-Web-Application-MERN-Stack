About Us

We are IKEA, a global leader in home furnishings. Founded in 1943, IKEA offers modern, affordable, and sustainable furniture and decor for every home.

Our History

IKEA was founded in 1943 by Ingvar Kamprad in Sweden. Over the years, it has grown to become a leading name in home furnishings worldwide. Our philosophy is to offer high-quality, functional products at prices that are affordable for the many people, not just the few.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a better everyday life for the many people by offering a wide variety of well-designed, functional products at affordable prices. We believe in a sustainable future for both people and the planet.
